What this plan does not include — and why

Topics such as Changing the Subject, Quadratic Graphs, Standard Form, Linear Inequalities, and Forming Complex Equations have been deliberately excluded. These require a level of abstract algebraic reasoning that should only be attempted once the 20 topics above are fully secure. Attempting them too early wastes revision time.

Once all 20 topics are consolidated, the next step would be: Sequences & nth Term, Straight Line Graphs, Basic Bearings, and Simple Ratio Sharing.
